Common questions

Buyer Questions Answered

Questions buyers frequently ask. Answered by AISHA, our AI property advisor.

Is Downtown Dubai freehold?

Yes. Downtown Dubai is a freehold zone. All nationalities can purchase with full DLD title. Emaar developed the majority of Downtown and maintains strict community standards.

What is the rental yield in Downtown Dubai?

Downtown yields 5–6% gross — lower than Marina or JVC due to high prices. However, capital appreciation is exceptional: +12.1% year-on-year.

Does Downtown qualify for Golden Visa?

Yes — almost all units qualify. Studios from AED 1.3M are below threshold, but 1-bedroom and above are typically AED 2M+.

Why are Downtown prices so high?

Supply is severely constrained — Downtown is built out with no significant new land. The Burj Khalifa, Dubai Mall, and Dubai Fountain create permanent scarcity premium.

Best developments in Downtown Dubai?

Emaar's Opera District (most prestigious), The Address Residences, Burj Vista, and 29 Boulevard are top choices. Opera District is most desired due to fountain views and direct Dubai Opera access.
